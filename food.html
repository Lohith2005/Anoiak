COFFEE

Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. It is one of the most consumed drinks globally and is known for its stimulating effects due to the presence of caffeine. Coffee comes in various forms, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, and more. It has a rich cultural history and is enjoyed for its diverse flavors, aromas, and social aspects.
